Recommended Gear

Below is the snowboarding gear that I use, and links to the products!

Snowboard

I bought a Chamonix board as it was an economical choice for my first experience. I recommend looking at www.thehouse.com for some great bargains.

Jacket

686 makes some great jackets that come with Approved GORE-TEX Poly Fabric +DWR

Helmet

Always wear a helmet. Just do it, You'll thank me later.

Boots

Burton is solid Snowboarding brand that has been making quality gear for a long time. These good fit great and keep you warm all day.

Bindings

I these binding by Flow becuase they are easier to get into because of their rear entry design.

Mittens

Gloves don't keep you as warm. I use a glove liner and then these bad boys to keep my hands warm. I always try to buy GORE TEX because of their reliability to keep me dry.

Googles

Buying a decent pair of Google is important. I found that fogging was a real issue when I bought a cheap pair. These were much better.

WAX

Waxing is a must to get decent speed and control. This one can be easily applied and is Plant Based.
